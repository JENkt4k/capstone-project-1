Forest Park Loop

Information about this trail:

  • Where to park: Pull up beside the curb on Lindell Boulevard between Skinker Boulevard and DeBaliviere Avenue.
  • Description: When you get out of the car, you will see a paved path when you step into Forest Park. Just past this path, you will notice a gravel path. This is the heels path. Follow this path and it will lead you around the park, finishing at your car.
  • Dog friendly: Yes
  • Difficulty level: Intermediate
