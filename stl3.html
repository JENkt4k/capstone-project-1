Tower Grove Park

Information about this trail:

  • Where to park: Enter the park at the intersection of Magnolia Avenue and Tower Grove Avenue. Park along the curb wherever you can fin a place in the interior of the park.
  • Description: When you get out of the car, you will see a paved path. Follow this along the perimeter of the park. You will finish at your car.
  • Dog friendly: Yes
  • Difficulty level: Easy
