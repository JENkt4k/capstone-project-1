Francis Park Loop

Information about this trail:

  • Where to park: Park in a spot along Tamm Avenue between Eichelberger St. and Nottingham Avenue.
  • Description: When you get out of the car, you will see a paved path when you step into Francis Park. Follow this path along the perimeter of the park and beside the tennis courts. You will finish when you are beside your car.
  • Dog friendly: Yes
  • Difficulty level: Easy
